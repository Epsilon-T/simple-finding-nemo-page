Finding Nemo

"Finding Nemo" is a charming animated film by Pixar, released in 2003. It follows Marlin, a clownfish, on his quest to find his son, Nemo, who has been captured by scuba divers. Along the way, Marlin teams up with Dory, a blue tang fish with short-term memory loss. Through their journey, Marlin faces his fears and learns important lessons about trust, courage, and family. The film highlights themes of hope and love, showcasing the strong bonds between family members. With its stunning animation and engaging story, "Finding Nemo" has become a beloved classic for audiences of all ages.
